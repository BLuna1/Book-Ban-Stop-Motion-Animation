The Book Ban Stop Motion Animation

The Book Ban Stop Motion Animation is about the affect and massive inpact that book bans have on our socity and how it restricts people of all ages from knowing the truth behind many historical events. Our animations shows how book bans affect a younger audience and how these bans restrict this young girl Kiki from being able to learn about different topics and ideas that are banned and removed from the publics eye. Kiki ultimatly overcomes her issue within the animation and shares the book that she was able to unban to more people.
